our mission

Our mission is to provide Resources Education Activities Community participation and Housing opportunities that will enable adults with developmental challenges to approximate the pattern of everyday living available to people without disabilities.

sports

R.E.A.C.H. West provides the coordination and support to the East Bay Buffaloes, one of the largest teams in Special Olympics Northern California

education

R.E.A.C.H. West provides training to parents, community partners and developmentally disabled community in West Costa and Northern Alameda counties

enrichment

Our Enrichment Programs are designed to develop skills for both personal growth and community enhancement. R.E.A.C.H. prides itself in giving back to the community
